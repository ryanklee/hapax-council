## §1. Scope and goal

GEAL is the expressive layer that makes the central Sierpinski triangle **unmistakably Hapax's avatar** on the livestream. It replaces HARDM, which is retired. GEAL is not a ward in the HOMAGE ward catalog; it is an expressive *extension* of the Sierpinski ward itself — the triangle does not gain an overlay, it *becomes* the avatar by extending its own grammar under three real-time signal channels:

1. **Voice** — Hapax speaking; sub-phoneme reactive
2. **Stance** — Hapax's posture (5-valued taxonomy from `shared/stimmung.py::Stance`)
3. **Grounding extrusions** — the non-facial equivalent of facial expressions: moments where Hapax's grounding in a specific context becomes visible

The ship criterion is the **pronoun test**: a naïve viewer watches the Sierpinski silently for 15 seconds, then is asked "what did you just see?" If the first pronoun is *thing* / *it* / *that entity*, GEAL ships. If the first pronoun is *visual* / *pattern* / *background*, GEAL fails. ≥ 2 of 3 naïve viewers must say *thing*.

## §2. Anti-anthropomorphization invariants (governance gate)

A GEAL variant ships only if all ten are TRUE. These are enforced by tests (§11).

1. No geometry in the upper half of the render forms two high-contrast spots with bilateral symmetry about the vertical axis (no eye-pair).
2. No horizontal feature in the lower half opens/closes synchronously with `vad_speech` or TTS emphasis (no mouth).
3. No 3×3 or larger cluster consistently lights in eyes-nose-mouth arrangement at any single location across a session.
4. Colour is signal-semantic (which signal is firing) — never affect-semantic (what Hapax is "feeling").
5. All transitions use continuous envelopes (sine, log-decay, ease); no step-functions faster than 200 ms.
6. No flesh-tone RGB ranges are reachable from any signal combination.
7. Every visible modulation traces to a grep-able signal in `/dev/shm/` or `shared/`. No hardcoded breathing / heartbeat / shimmer sines.
8. No glyph or path spells "Hapax" or uses gendered pronouns; no personality nouns in comments or labels.
9. Scale-up means more cells / density / complexity activated, NEVER more-expressive cells (ANT primary-framing rule).
10. A naïve viewer with no context reads "system signal display" / "algorithmic pattern" / "entity" — never "character" / "face".

## §3. Aesthetic invariants (ship gate)

Five positive invariants (what must be true of every GEAL output) and seven refusals (what must not appear). Derived from the non-face entity canon; see the round-1 research doc §3 for the reference walk.

**Must have:**

1. **Intentional asymmetry under symmetric law.** Sierpinski is the law; GEAL's expression makes willful-looking choices inside it.
2. **A non-facial point-of-attention** (phase origin, density centroid, recursion seed) — *where the entity is looking from*, without eyes.
3. **Response latency with shape.** Three-phase curves on every reactive event: anticipate (80–200 ms) → commit (60–120 ms) → settle (400–900 ms). No linear reactivity.
4. **Internal state leakage.** Motion the viewer catches that wasn't *for* them (idle-state slow recursion-edge accretion, stance pre-transition settling, pre-grounding-latch trace).
5. **Scale-coherent self-similarity.** Every GEAL primitive holds at every depth level. Zoom preserves the grammar.

**Must not have:**

- Particle-cloud haze (Anadol-trap / screensaver read).
- Colour-as-emotion mapping.
- Radial-symmetry cliché (mandala / lotus read).
- Beat-synced pulse-to-volume (HARDM failure; VU-meter read).
- Text-in-a-box overlays inside the triangle (that is the GEM ward's job).
- Glitch-VHS effects (dated, non-Sierpinski grammar).
- Linear reactivity (amplitude → brightness mapping — decoration tell).

## §4. Geometric vocabulary — what "extending Sierpinski" means

The Sierpinski renderer today draws a 2-level recursion: 1 root + 3 L1 corners + 1 L1 centre void + 9 L2 corner sub-triangles = 13 triangles. GEAL extends this to **baseline L3, max-under-activation L4**, with a per-level rendering law.

### §4.1. Depth policy

- **Baseline depth = L3** (40 triangles, 240 edges, ≈ 10 ms / tick Cairo on 1280×720). Three semantic layers of recursion are required for the three-channel architecture (L1 = vertex halos / outer grammar, L2 = sub-triangle ignition, L3 = cross-level cascades & idle accretion).
- **Max under activation = L4** (121 triangles, ≈ 30 ms / tick). L4 is the ceiling for SEEKING stance or imagination-converge grounding events. L4 activation is held ≤ 400 ms and returns, matching §3 curve discipline.
- **L5 is the coherence cliff.** Smallest edge at L5 on a 75%-height triangle ≈ 19 px; the 8 px stroke halo merges adjacent edges into textured haze. L5 not reachable in v1.
- **L3/L4 are not rendered inside the three YT inscribed rects.** Rendered only in the three slivers (L2 corner minus inscribed rect) and in the centre-void interior. This is the load-bearing rule that keeps video unoccluded.

### §4.2. Per-level rendering law

Stroke width and opacity taper geometrically with depth; glow disappears past L3.

| Level | Core stroke (px) | Glow stroke (px) | Core alpha | Glow alpha |
|-------|-----------------:|-----------------:|-----------:|-----------:|
| L0 root | 2.0 | 6.0 | 0.80 | 0.15 |
| L1 | 1.5 | 4.5 | 0.80 | 0.15 |
| L2 | 1.25 | 3.0 | 0.70 | 0.10 |
| L3 | 1.0 | 1.8 | 0.55 | 0.06 |
| L4 (activation only) | 0.75 | *(none)* | 0.35 | — |

Audio-reactive stroke bump (`+ energy × 1.5`) applies to L0–L2 only. L3/L4 stay structural — no audio muddying of the recursion grammar.

### §4.3. Fundamentals that must be preserved

Any primitive that does not honour all five fails governance and does not ship:

a. **Dyadic self-similarity.** Divide-by-midpoint is the only legal subdivision move.
b. **Edge-not-fill primacy.** Lines ARE the object. Voids are load-bearing.
c. **Discrete recursion levels.** L0 / L1 / L2 / L3 / L4 are semantic. No smoothly interpolated "depth 2.4".
d. **Legal positions only.** Vertices, edges, midpoints, centroids anchor. Free points permitted only via chaos-game (algorithmically legal) — and v1 does not use chaos-game.
e. **Three-fold rotational symmetry with centre-void as negative space.**

## §5. Render-pipeline architecture — 8-layer stack

Painted bottom → top inside the Sierpinski triangle. Cairo `OPERATOR_OVER` on layers 0–5 and 8; `OPERATOR_ADD` (additive) on layers 6–7 so they *never occlude*.

| # | Layer | Blend | Clip | Hosts |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| 0 | Background clear | replace | canvas | (transparent — main FX chain handles bed) |
| 1 | Structural shadow / void wash | over, α ≤ 0.20 | L1 centre, L2 non-corner slivers | optional stance-driven wash; off by default in v1 |
| 2 | YT video rects (inscribed 16:9) | over, α driven by `_slot_opacity` | per corner inscribed rect | YT frame only |
| 3 | Chladni sub-triangle ignition (V1) + latch-and-fade (G2) | over, α 0.0–0.55 | 9 L2 sub-triangles **minus inscribed rect** (the slivers) | V1 fills, G2 accents |
| 4 | Centre-void field | over, α 0.0–0.85 | L1 centre triangle | V1 centre ignition, G1 wavefront seed, G3 centroid pulse |
| 5 | Structural Sierpinski lines | over, α per §4.2 | none (strokes) | existing line work; S1 depth gates L3/L4 edge visibility; V3 edge-weight; G1 brightness packet |
| 6 | Vertex halos (V2, G6) | **plus (additive)**, α 0.0–0.7 radial | soft-clipped to halo bbox | V2 F0 halos, G6 gaze-hop marker |
| 7 | Apex-of-origin wavefronts (G1) | **plus (additive)**, α 0.0–0.5 travelling gaussian | follows edge polylines | G1 200–800 ms brightness packet |
| 8 | Idle accretion marks | over, α 0.0–0.3 | recursion-tree edges | Verostko-style sub-marks; suppressed during voice / grounding events |

**Centre-void decision: the existing waveform is retired.** Waveform's audio-energy information is preserved through existing stroke-width modulation on layers 5; V1 Chladni ignition + G1 wavefront seed + G3 centroid pulse are higher-order reads of the same signal.

### §5.1. `video_attention` scalar

Published to `/dev/shm/hapax-compositor/video-attention.f32` by the Sierpinski source each tick. Read by GEAL and by future WGSL parity node.

```
video_attention = max(slot_opacity[0..N-1]) * frame_freshness
frame_freshness = 1.0 if frame_mtime < 2 s else exponential decay
```

GEAL multiplies its activation budget by `(1.0 − 0.7 × video_attention)`. Featured video active → GEAL pulls back to ~30 % activation. Video idle → GEAL runs at full activation. GEAL never fills an empty video rect.

## §6. Three expression channels

Each channel is a function of signals → state → primitives, mediated by §7 temporal curves and §8 palette integration.

### §6.1. Channel 1 — Voice

**Signals (via `/dev/shm/`):**

- `hapax-compositor/voice-state.json` — `{tts_active, operator_speech_active}` — live today.
- `hapax-compositor/homage-voice-register.json` — register enum — live today.
- `compositor._cached_audio["mixer_energy"]` — live today, ~10 ms lag.
- `hapax-daimonion/tts-envelope.f32` — **new in Phase 2** — ~100 Hz mmap ring of (rms, spectral_centroid, zcr, f0, voicing_probability). Written by `tts_envelope_publisher.py` in CpalRunner.

**Primitives (v1 MVP bold):**

- **V1 — Chladni sub-triangle ignition.** `spectral_centroid × rms` selects 1–3 of the 13 L2 sub-triangle slivers to *go still* (filled, opaque) while the rest dither. High centroid → apex triangles ignite. Low centroid → base triangles. Fill saturation = intensity. SecondOrderLP on the voice envelope (ω ≈ 12 Hz, ζ ≈ 0.7) keeps the response smooth.
- **V2 — Vertex halos at F0.** Three primary vertices each carry a soft additive radial halo. Halo radius = F0 mapped through LP filter (ω ≈ 8 Hz, ζ ≈ 0.8). Opacity = voicing probability through log-decay tail (τ ≈ 600 ms). Vertex identity encodes register (apex = announcing; left = conversing; right = textmode / choral / ritual).
- V3 — edge-weight as voicing × envelope (Phase 3).
- V4 — phoneme-glyph accumulation in centre void (Phase 3; requires Kokoro phoneme hook).
- V5 — deferred.
- V6 — sweep-scan sampler (Phase 3).
- V7 — coherence jitter (Phase 3).

### §6.2. Channel 2 — Stance

**Signal:** `/dev/shm/hapax-dmn/stimmung.json::overall_stance` — 5-valued enum: `NOMINAL`, `SEEKING`, `CAUTIOUS`, `DEGRADED`, `CRITICAL`. Transitions are infrequent (seconds – minutes).

**Primitives (v1 MVP bold):**

- **S1 — Recursive-depth breathing.** The most highly converged primitive across round-1 agents (three independent directions landed on it). Stance → max depth:

  | Stance | Max depth | Character |
  |--------|:---------:|---|
  | NOMINAL | L3 | balanced, breathing |
  | SEEKING | L4 | reaches outward; outward probes into voids |
  | CAUTIOUS | L2 | defensive simplification; held posture |
  | DEGRADED | L2 | depth collapse |
  | CRITICAL | L1 | outline only |

  Stance → depth crossfade on three_phase envelope (anticipate 120 ms, commit 90 ms, settle 600 ms, settle_tau 400 ms). Depth is integer; crossfade animates per-level alpha during the transition.

- **S2 — Apex-weight redistribution.** Three corners carry weights summing to 1.0, modulating sub-triangle luminance per branch:

  | Stance | Top | BL | BR |
  |--------|:---:|:--:|:--:|
  | NOMINAL | 0.33 | 0.33 | 0.33 |
  | SEEKING | 0.55 | 0.22 | 0.22 |
  | CAUTIOUS | 0.15 | 0.42 | 0.42 |
  | DEGRADED | 0.60 | 0.20 | 0.20 (one-corner collapse) |
  | CRITICAL | (all three dark; mid-edges glow) | | |

  `three_phase` envelope (180 / 120 / 800, peak Δ 0.22, settle_tau 500 ms).

- S3 — chromatic register swap (palette role permutation) — handled by §8.
- S4 — edge-tension jitter (Phase 3).
- S5 — Cantor-dust density (Phase 3).
- S6 — slow whole-triangle rotation (Phase 3, pending operator taste).

### §6.3. Channel 3 — Grounding extrusions

**Signal:** `DirectorIntent.grounding_provenance` list deltas (new citations per tick). Source-class → apex mapping:

| Source class | Apex |
|---|---|
| Operator perception (InsightFace, Pi NoIR, room-change) | top |
| RAG / memory / vault-note retrieval | bottom-left |
| Chat / world / music-match / SoundCloud | bottom-right |
| Imagination-converge (cross-source corroboration) | all three, collapsing inward |

**Primitives (v1 MVP bold):**

- **G1 — Wavefront ripple from apex-of-origin.** A gaussian brightness packet travels from the source apex across the recursion tree. Packet σ ≈ 120 ms; total travel 600 ms. Carried along edge polylines via pre-cached traversal paths. Additive blend (layer 7). Reads as source-class from geometry alone — the viewer learns the three-apex grammar within minutes of exposure.
- **G2 — Sub-triangle latch-and-fade.** A single depth-N sub-triangle is selected per extrusion via `hash(source_id) % num_cells_at_depth`, latched at full accent colour for 150–300 ms (commit), then decays over 1000–1500 ms (settle, log-decay τ ≈ 450 ms). Repeated recognitions of the same source_id → same cell → viewer learns "that cell = that thing".
- G3 — centroid pulse on surprise (Phase 3; low-probability chat / impingement events).
- G4 — edge-trace reach on retrieval (Phase 3).
- G5 — depth-pulse on imagination-converge (Phase 3; depth +2 for 400 ms then return).
- G6 — perception-anchor hop (Phase 3; Pi NoIR gaze tracker).

## §7. Temporal curve library

New module `shared/geal_curves.py`. Pure Python, stdlib `math` only, ~130 lines. Two families, one unified `Envelope` API.

### §7.1. Event-bound (three-phase) curves

```
three_phase(t_ms, anticipate_ms, commit_ms, settle_ms,
            anticipate_amp=-0.10, peak_amp=1.0, settle_tau_ms=300.0)
```

Three segments stitched:

- **anticipate:** `easeOutQuad` into a counter-direction move of magnitude `anticipate_amp × peak_amp` (Disney squash-before-stretch lineage).
- **commit:** critically-damped overshoot `1 − (1−p)·exp(−3p)`, landing on `peak_amp` with ~5–15 % overshoot (from Game Programming Gems damped-spring family).
- **settle:** log-decay tail `exp(−dt / settle_tau_ms)` — asymmetric fade is the "grace" signature that distinguishes entity from decoration.

`gaussian_pulse(t_ms, center_ms, sigma_ms, peak_amp=1.0)` for G1 spatial-travelling packets.

`log_decay(t_ms, tau_ms, peak_amp=1.0)` as a settle-only primitive.

### §7.2. Signal-bound (second-order low-pass)

```
class SecondOrderLP:
    omega: float          # natural frequency rad/s
    zeta: float           # damping ratio
    value: float
    velocity: float

    def tick(self, now_s: float, target: float) -> float: ...
```

Critically-damped (ζ = 0.7–0.9) filter driving the continuous signals: voice envelope → V1 fill, F0 → V2 radius, voicing → V2 opacity. Shape response without ever approaching a step function.

### §7.3. Unified `Envelope` wrapper

One API for both families:

```
@dataclass
class Envelope:
    kind: CurveKind   # THREE_PHASE | GAUSSIAN_PULSE | LOG_DECAY | SECOND_ORDER_LP
    fire_at_s: float | None = None
    params: dict = field(default_factory=dict)

    def tick(self, now_s: float, signal: float | None = None) -> float: ...
    def is_expired(self, now_s: float, grace_s: float = 0.3) -> bool: ...
```

### §7.4. Composition

Multiple envelopes writing the same primitive attribute compose via:

- Default **ADD** with clamp. Event-bound envelopes stack additively (G2 accent latch rides on V1 fill).
- **REPLACE** when the same primitive-slot re-fires within 80 ms of a previous fire (older envelope cancelled; new one takes over from the current interpolated value to preserve continuity).
- Signal-bound envelopes **never stack** — exactly one filter per (primitive, slot).
- **MAX** mode available for peak-of-peaks rendering (rare — reserved for CRITICAL-stance edge glow).

### §7.5. Per-primitive curve assignment

| Primitive | Curve | Params |
|-----------|-------|--------|
| V1 Chladni ignition | SecondOrderLP | ω=12 Hz, ζ=0.7 |
| V2 vertex halo radius | SecondOrderLP | ω=8 Hz, ζ=0.8 |
| V2 vertex halo opacity | LogDecay settle | τ=600 ms |
| S1 depth crossfade | ThreePhase | 120 / 90 / 600, amp=±0.1 depth, τ=400 ms |
| S2 apex-weight | ThreePhase | 180 / 120 / 800, amp=0.22, τ=500 ms |
| G1 wavefront packet | GaussianPulse (spatial) | σ=120 ms, peak=1.0, travel 600 ms |
| G2 sub-triangle latch | ThreePhase | 100 / 80 / 1200, τ=450 ms log-decay |

## §8. Palette integration

GEAL retires the hardcoded synthwave palette cycle in `sierpinski_renderer.py` and drives colour from the palette family registry (`shared/palette_registry.py`).

### §8.1. Stance → palette (primary mapping)

Signal-semantic, not affect-semantic. Severity is rendered as reduced chroma + dropped lightness, not "red = angry".

| Stance | Primary | Fallback | Rationale |
|--------|---------|----------|-----------|
| NOMINAL | `cool-mist` | `silver-fog` | Declared default; carries HOMAGE A6 cyan invariant forward. |
| SEEKING | `xenon-pulse` | `sage-morning` | `pulsing` + high chroma = reaching outward. Not alarm. |
| CAUTIOUS | `sage-morning` | `wintergreen` | `breathing` + low saturation + clip_s_curve softens extremes = held posture. |
| DEGRADED | `silver-fog` | `monochrome` | Near-zero chroma = system admitting it cannot render with full vocabulary. |
| CRITICAL | `monochrome` | `bruise` | Pure grayscale + preserve_luminance = legibility-first. Severity by chroma subtraction. |

Stance-transition crossfade uses `three_phase` (300–400 ms) between two pre-resolved LAB outputs — the active palette's LAB anchors are cached; GEAL interpolates LAB→sRGB in the transition window.

Stance→palette mapping lives in `shared/geal_stance_palette_map.yaml` (operator-editable, not hardcoded).

### §8.2. Register → halo role assignment (within-palette modulation)

Register does not change palette; register permutes how the active palette's roles (`dominant_lab`, `accent_lab`) map to the three vertex halos.

| Register | Apex halo | BL halo | BR halo | Overall halo α |
|----------|-----------|---------|---------|:--:|
| announcing | dominant | accent @ 0.4 chroma | accent @ 0.4 chroma | +0.3 |
| conversing | duotone(dominant, accent) on voicing | duotone(…) | duotone(…) | baseline |
| textmode | (off) | accent | (off) | baseline |
| choral | accent | dominant | accent | baseline |
| ritual | dominant | dominant | dominant | +0.5, lowered ω on LP |

### §8.3. Grounding extrusion → accent draw, not palette swap

Extrusion-latch colour is drawn from the *current active palette's* anchors — never triggers a palette swap:

| Apex | Colour |
|------|--------|
| top (operator-perception) | `dominant_lab` |
| bottom-left (RAG / memory) | `accent_lab` |
| bottom-right (chat / world) | `_lerp_lab(dominant, accent, 0.5)` pre-computed via `apply_palette` |
| imagination-converge (all three) | three apices fire respective colours simultaneously |

### §8.4. Chains as ambient substrate

Palette chains (`day-arc`, `nocturne`, `electric-study` from the registry) run as the ambient substrate driven by wall-clock + working-mode affinity hint. Stance override takes precedence and re-targets the chain's current step on return to NOMINAL via 400 ms crossfade. Operator or programme can force-chain via `PaletteChainRunner.force_chain(chain_id)` — not exposed as a primary surface in v1.

### §8.5. Primary curve modes used

From `shared/palette_curve_evaluator.py`:

- **duotone** — V2 vertex halos. `stop_low = accent`, `stop_high = dominant`, L* from halo falloff. Luminance-preserving, emissive-safe.
- **gradient_map** — V1 Chladni sub-triangle fill. Stops keyed on `spectral_centroid × rms`.
- **lab_shift** — S2 apex-weight redistribution (ΔL per sub-triangle).
- **hue_rotate** — stance-transition animation only (8–18° sweep over 300 ms; not a steady state).
- `channel_mix` / `identity` — not used in v1.

### §8.6. Backwards-compatibility

`sierpinski_renderer.py`'s hardcoded `COLORS` tuple is removed **after** Phase 1 ships and clears §2/§3 gates. Until then GEAL is ward-gated (`HAPAX_GEAL_ENABLED=1`); the non-GEAL branch keeps `COLORS` for rollback. Post-acceptance, `COLORS` is deleted and a golden PNG per (stance × register) cell is committed to `tests/goldens/geal/`.

## §9. Render pipeline — Cairo-first, WGSL Phase 4 parallel (never replacement)

The compositor has two Sierpinski rendering paths today:

- **Cairo path** on the pre-FX `cairooverlay` — active when camera is the composited source.
- **WGSL path** in `agents/shaders/nodes/sierpinski_content.wgsl` — active when Reverie is the composited source.

Both exist today. Both render Sierpinski. GEAL inherits the bifurcation:

- **Phase 1 ships Cairo GEAL only.** Phase 1 is 1–2 days of work. Cairo wins on: (a) iteration speed (edit Python → autoreload → see on livestream in seconds; WGSL is naga-validate → plan.json → Rust reload, 5–15 s + harder debug); (b) visible-time coverage (camera-primary is the default state; Reverie-active is a minority state during stance-driven dreamy passages); (c) §3 aesthetic bar — per-sub-triangle discrete-recursive colour application IS the aesthetic; Cairo handles it natively.
- **Phase 4 ships WGSL parity**, not replacement. When Reverie becomes the composited source, WGSL-GEAL lights up; when camera-primary, Cairo-GEAL lights up. The ward-render-scope gate selects. Both implementations stay forever — exactly as Sierpinski itself works today.

This is not optional complexity. It is a structural property of the compositor's two compositing paths.

## §11. Governance — tests + runtime guards

Enforcement for §2 and §3. GEAL PR CI must run these and pass.

1. **Anti-personification linter extension.** Add `geal_geometry` rule family to `shared/anti_personification_linter.py`: regex-reject files matching `agents/studio_compositor/geal_*.py` that contain `eye`, `mouth`, `face`, `brow`, `smile`, `blink`, `wink`, `breath`, `heartbeat` outside an explicit refusal comment.
2. **Bilateral-symmetry runtime guard.** Unit test rasterises GEAL output at 8 representative states and asserts no bilateral-symmetry hotspot exceeds threshold in the upper half (autocorrelation across vertical axis; fail if mirror-correlation > 0.85 for high-luminance cells).
3. **Blink-floor test.** For every GEAL output channel, assert no discrete event has rise / fall time < 200 ms. Mechanical — tests sample the curve library at 1 ms and checks `|dv/dt| < 1/0.2 s`.
4. **Flesh-tone refusal test.** Assert no combination of signals produces RGB with R > G > B and R/B > 1.3 and G/B > 1.1 outside the accent-yellow range.
5. **Shimmer-constant linter.** Reject literal `sin(...)` / `cos(...)` multipliers on colour channels not sourced from stimmung or signal payload.
6. **Visual-regression goldens.** Golden PNG per (stance × register) cell committed to `tests/goldens/geal/`. Operator signs off on goldens before merge.
7. **Pronoun-test acceptance gate.** Show rendering to 3 naïve viewers × 15 s silent; ≥ 2 must say *thing* / *entity*. Retry with redesign (not patch) on failure.
8. **§2 invariant #10 narrative check.** PR description must explicitly identify which §2 invariants the change could have broken and how the tests above cover that risk.

## §15. Aesthetic acceptance — what "astoundingly beautiful" looks like when shipped

- The Sierpinski feels *present* — a viewer with no context describes it as *something* watching, not decoration.
- Stance shifts visibly and immediately (<400 ms) but do not demand attention.
